ABOUT OF BHU COLLAGE

Banaras Hindu Universitylisten (help·info) (BHU) (Hindi: काशी हिन्दू विश्वविद्यालय IAST:kashi Hindu vishwavidyalaya IPA:
kaʃi hind̪u viʃvəvid̪yaləy), formerly Central Hindu College, is a collegiate, central, and
research university located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was
established in 1916 jointly by Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya, the Maharaja of Darbhanga Rameshwar Singh, Maharaja of Banaras Prabhu
Narayan Singh, Sunder Lal and British Theosophist and Home Rule
League founder Annie Besant. With over 30,000 students, and 18,000 residing on campus, BHU is the largest residential university in
Asia.[6][7] The university is one of the eight public institutions declared as an Institute of Eminence by the Government of India.[8]
BHU has often been referred by different names throughout the history and present. Some of the English
names include Banaras University,[9][10] Benares Hindu University, and Hindu Univ
